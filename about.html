About Us:

WASI aims to make it easier for people living in Jordan to order items from abroad. Anyone living in Jordan knows what a hassle it is to order from abroad. First, it takes months for the delivery to arrive. Second, shipping costs are so high; sometimes higher than the price of the order itself! Third, it takes too long for the order to arrive, sometimes months. Lastly, you still have to pay the customs once your item arrives to Jordan. With WASI, these hurdles are avoided, and a better solution is put in place. Requesters can have travellers bring their orders on their way back to Jordan, and travellers can make some extra cash on their travels!

Benefits of using WASI:

Save money when ordering!
Save time on shipping
Make money during your travels
100% guaranteed satisfaction!
