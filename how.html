How it Works:

Traveller:

Select an item you can carry and deliver by the deadline, and confirm you will bring it..

Bring the item with you when you return to Jordan.

Meet with and give the requester the item.

Earn Some Money!

Requester:

Add an item that you want specifying the date you want it by, where it can be bought, how much it costs, and how much you’d pay the traveller.

Enter your credit card info. Price will be deducted only after you receive your item.

Wait for a traveller to confirm the request.

Meet with the traveller and recieve your item!
